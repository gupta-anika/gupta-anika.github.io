Biotech Value Creation

During my junior year at MIT, I spent some time at the Venture Capital firm Flagship Pioneering, in their innovation arm. Working with their in-house company Cobalt (then VL39), I learned about the series of steps in the biotech product development pipeline and identified key steps to increase the probability of success of their product.

Orphan Drugs

Orphan drugs treat conditions that affect less than 200,000 patients. Often, these are the only therapies on the market for such rare conditions and are thus highly valued. The FDA has measures in place to facilitate orphan drug development, as is visible by the smaller clinical trial sizes required and shorter timelines for development.
